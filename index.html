About

Jisung Kang is a Korean judge seconded to the HCCH, working at the intersection of AI, digital assets, CBDC, and private international law. Focused on transforming complex legal standards into explainable and operational AI-assisted tools while enhancing judicial independence, accessibility, and human rights.
